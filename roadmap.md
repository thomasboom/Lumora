## Core Features to Add

**1. Pacing & Activity Budgeting**
- Track energy "spend" vs. recovery
- Warn when approaching energy limits
- Suggest rest periods based on patterns

**2. PEM (Post-Exertional Malaise) Tracking**
- 24-48 hour delayed symptom logging
- Link symptoms back to previous activities
- Identify trigger activities

**3. Symptom Severity**
- Add intensity slider (1-10) for each symptom
- Track duration of symptoms
- Better visualize symptom patterns

**4. Personalized Insights**
- "What worked this week?" summaries
- Trigger detection (e.g., "Exercise often causes brain fog 24h later")
- Recovery trends over time

**5. Recovery Goals**
- Set gradual activity increase goals
- Celebrate milestones
- Progress graphs

**6. Daily Check-in Reminders**
- Gentle notifications to track
- Suggested optimal logging times

**7. Doctor Export**
- Generate PDF reports of symptoms/trends
- Weekly/monthly summaries for medical visits

**8. Activity Impact Scoring**
- Rate each activity's energy cost
- Learn your personal "spoon theory" budget
- Warn before high-energy activities

**9. Custom Symptoms**
- Add your own Long Covid symptoms
- Log duration/intensity

**10. Wearable Integration** (advanced)
- Resting heart rate correlation
- Sleep quality tracking
- Activity data import
